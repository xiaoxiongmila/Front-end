JSDigester Test Harness


This is a quick test of JSDigester. It will parse the following XML:

<movies numMovies="2">
 <movie>
    <title>Star Wars</title>
    <actor gender="male">Harrison Ford</actor>
    <actor gender="female">Carrie Fisher</actor>
  </movie>
  <movie>
    <title>Real Genius</title>
    <actor gender="male">Val Kilmer</actor>
  </movie>
</movies>



(Note: processing could take some time, 30-45 seconds perhaps, and your browser will lock up during that time, all because of the logging JSDigester does. To have it run at full speed, comment out the call to jsDigester.setLogger() in testScript.js).
